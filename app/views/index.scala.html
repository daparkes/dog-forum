Welcome to the Pig Game!!

A game for everyone!

How to play

  • • Read the rules before you start the game.  
  • • Click the button below to begin.
  • • Players have to input their names before the start of the game.
  • • Both players decide on an end point and input the value (must be equal to or greater than 50).
  • • The 'New Game' button has to be clicked to start the game.
  • • Enjoy the game!!

Rules

  • • Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a dice until either a 1 is rolled or the player decides to "hold"
  • • If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • • If the player rolls any other number, it is added to their turn total and the player's turn continues.
  • • If a player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to their score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • • The first player to reach the end point wins!
